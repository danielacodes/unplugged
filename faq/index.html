Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

All you need to do is to send us an email at retreat@unplugged.com. We will contact you back and tell you what we can do for you.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Each team! :) Our experience shows that it's better to come to retreat in groups not bigger than 15 people. It's important that you will fell close and your team will be able to connect.

What size teams can you accomodate?

We can accomodate 23 people. We have 12 bedrooms - 3 x 1 person, 7 x 2 person, 2 x 3 person.

Is there really no wifi available?

Wifi is available but we encourage our guests to spend time together with their teams instead of their phones ;)
